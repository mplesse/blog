What is ‘sip of magic’? It’s the aesthetic of sitting down with a cup of coffee or tea, opening the rustling pages of a book. Leaving stains on a page and not minding, because this moment is not about perfection. It’s about escaping, breathing, being yourself without having to explain. It’s 20-something years old with the pressure of being able to become anything you want. It’s the motivation to change your life or the satisfaction of knowing you’ve arrived. It’s another sip. SIP OF MAGIC a collection of memories in the making
